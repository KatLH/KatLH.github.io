MAKE THE SWAP

- LIVING WASTE FREE -




In the United States, a single human being produces an average 31.36 pounds of waste each week.

You can significantly reduce this number by making a few changes to your lifestyle.


Click on the Swap! button to learn how you can reduce the amount of waste you produce with these everyday items!




Swap out disposable plastic razors for a reuasble stainless steel razor.


Replace disposable pads and tampons with a reusable silicone cup or cloth pads


Buy bread and other baked goods from the grocery's bakery and store it in a canvas bread bag


Instead of using disposable plastic utensils for picnics and on-the-go lunches, take a set of reusable bamboo utensils.


Plastic straws are harmful to the evironment and our oceans, replace them with reusable glass or stainless steal straws. (Or don't use straws at all!)


When cleaning your face, rather than using disposable cotton balls, replace them with reusable cotton rounds.
